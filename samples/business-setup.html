Sample workflow

Business setup and PRO inquiries.

Qualify company formation leads by jurisdiction, activity, visa needs, bank support, and timeline before a consultant spends time manually.

Mainland / free zone Visa needs Bank support

Lead sources

  • WhatsApp inquiry from website or ads.
  • Consultation request form.
  • Email from a founder comparing packages.
  • Referral message from an existing client.

First qualification questions

  • Mainland, free zone, offshore, or unsure?
  • Business activity and number of shareholders?
  • Visa or residency needs?
  • Bank account support required?
  • Timeline and budget range?
Priority Signal Draft next action
Hot Clear activity, timeline under 30 days, ready for consultation. Offer consultation slots and ask for missing license details.
Warm Researching options, unclear jurisdiction. Ask business activity and visa needs before package discussion.
Low fit No timeline, price-only, vague request. Send a short clarifying response and avoid consultant overload.
