Draft reply for owner approval

Hi, yes. A starter setup can organize clinic inquiries without moving you into a heavy CRM.

To scope it correctly, I would need:

  • Where inquiries arrive now: WhatsApp, website form, Instagram, email, or ads.
  • Which fields matter before booking: treatment, date, returning patient, urgency, budget, language.
  • Who approves replies and who updates the status after a booking.

After that, the starter workflow can be prepared with a lead tracker, status rules, reply templates, and daily summary format.

Not auto-sent

Owner approval gate

The starter setup is intentionally controlled. AI helps structure and draft; the business owner or coordinator decides what leaves the company account.

  • Review summary and priority.
  • Edit or approve the reply draft.
  • Send from the real business channel.
  • Update CRM status after response, booking, payment, or loss.
